About me

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow with the Department of Electrical Engineering and  Centre for Complexity and Complex Networks, City University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Guanrong Chen. I was a postdoctoral fellow with the Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Victoria from Jul. 2024 to Sept. 2024, working with Prof. Yang Shi, and with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Windsor from Sept. 2021 to Jul. 2024, working with Prof. Xiang Chen. I received my Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from the East China University of Science and Technology in Jun. 2021, supervised by Prof. Yang Tang. I was a visiting Ph.D. student with the Department of Electrical Engineering, University of Notre Dame from Oct. 2019 to Oct. 2020, supervised by Prof. Vijay Gupta. My research interest includes networked feedback control, multi-objective control, time-delay systems, and mean-field games.
